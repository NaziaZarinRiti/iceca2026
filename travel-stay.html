Travel & Stay

To ensure a smooth experience at ICECA 2026, please take note of the following travel and accommodation arrangements.

Self-Arrangement

All participants, including students and professors, are responsible for their own lodging and transportation arrangements.

Assistance

For help finding hotels or travel options in Chattogram, please contact the logistics team at ICECA@auw.edu.bd. A suggested location for Hotel Agrabad is provided in the map section below.

Field Trip Transport

Registered participants selecting the trip package will be provided transport to the designated field trip site. Selected participants will be informed and updated through email.

Conference Venue

Sonapahar Farmhouse Resort, Rangunia, Chattogram, Bangladesh.
